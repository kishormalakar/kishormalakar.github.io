About

I am a civil servant who handles logistics and materials management for the Ministry of Railways, Government of India. Indian Railways being a mammoth organization, needs a lot materials to keep its operation running. My job is to ensure that the executives responsible for various operations get their materials in time without any compromise in quality. My day to day activities include lots of interaction with suppliers, contractors, vendors and service providers. I am always keen to solve the problems they are facing while dealing with the government so that it gets little easier for everyone to do business with the government.

My prior experiences also include working as a Sr. Operations Officer for Indian Oil Corporation Ltd. It was an wonderful opportunity for professional growth. Our work was very effective and very vital in ensuring the availability of this essential commodity during the nationwide lockdown of 2020.
